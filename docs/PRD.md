---

1. Executive Summary

CareerPilot AI is an Agentic AI-powered career mentoring platform designed to help students and professionals achieve their career goals through personalized guidance.

Unlike traditional career guidance platforms, CareerPilot AI uses multiple specialized AI agents that collaborate to analyze the user's profile, identify skill gaps, recommend learning resources, suggest projects, conduct mock interviews, optimize resumes, and continuously monitor progress.

The primary objective is to provide users with a single intelligent platform capable of guiding them throughout their entire career journey.

---

2. Product Overview

CareerPilot AI functions as a virtual career mentor rather than a traditional chatbot.

The platform collects information about the user's education, skills, experience, interests, and career aspirations.

Using this information, multiple AI agents work together to generate personalized recommendations and structured career roadmaps.

The system continuously adapts to the user's progress and provides updated recommendations as the user develops new skills.

---

3. Problem Statement

Career preparation is currently fragmented across multiple platforms.

Students use different applications for coding practice, online learning, networking, resume creation, interview preparation, and project management.

This forces users to manually plan their learning journey without personalized guidance.

CareerPilot AI addresses this challenge by integrating these activities into one intelligent mentoring platform.

The primary goals of CareerPilot AI are:

- Help users identify skill gaps.
- Generate customized learning roadmaps.
- Recommend practical projects.

7. Functional Requirements

The system shall allow users to:

- Register and log in securely.
- Create and manage personal profiles.
- Define career goals.
- Upload resumes.
- Analyze skill gaps.
- Generate personalized learning roadmaps.
- Receive project recommendations.
- Chat with AI career mentors.
- Participate in AI mock interviews.
- Track progress through dashboards.
- Receive personalized notifications.

---

8. Non-Functional Requirements

The system should:

- Be scalable.
- Be secure.
- Be responsive.
- Support mobile and web platforms.
- Provide fast AI responses.
- Maintain user privacy.
- Be modular and maintainable.
